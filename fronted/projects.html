The Concept

You find yourself alone in a classroom at night, fighting to survive 50 minutes. The game combines puzzles, resource management, and strategic choices that determine your survival.

Gameplay Mechanics

  • Hold doors against threats
  • Uncover secrets through careful observation
  • Use surveillance cameras to detect danger
  • Manage resources and make tough choices

5 Nights, Rising Difficulty

Each night brings new challenges and adversaries. The difficulty rises continuously until the final night, where everything comes together.
